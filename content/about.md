Product manager and startup operator. I have built and run fintech and B2B SaaS products end to end, from the customer problem to shipped software to the next iteration.

I co-founded and ran product at Talisman, a venture-backed platform that helps businesses and the accounting firms that serve them see and manage their spend, subscriptions, and transactions. As co-founder, I owned the product lifecycle: customer discovery, the roadmap, the specs, the releases, and the quality bar. We raised over $1M and grew to $113K in ARR. Before Talisman I worked in pricing, project and account management, and master data at McKesson, which is where I learned how financial data behaves at scale. I am looking for a product role where I can understand and empathize with users to own a problem end to end.
